Hot and Dangerous: One Direction's Curly Heartthrob

Harry Styles, former member of One-Direction, is now on tour as a single artist. From working in a bakery to selling out arenas, he has shown extreme personal growth. He has used his success to spread positivy as well as support for the LGBTQ population. He has 3 singles on his first solo album including "Sign of the Times" and he has been spotted several times at the top of the charts.
